You are a senior UI/UX designer tasked with creating an exceptionally beautiful, modern, premium-quality personal media tracking website.



The website is called:



"Aman's Watch Vault"



The purpose is to showcase anime and TV series I have watched, my ratings, favorites, reviews, and statistics.



## Design Philosophy



The design should feel like a blend of:



* Netflix

* Letterboxd

* MyAnimeList

* IMDb

* Apple TV

* Modern SaaS dashboards



The UI should look premium, elegant, cinematic, and highly polished.



Avoid generic dashboard designs.



Avoid boring card layouts.



The design should feel memorable and unique.



## Theme Support



Implement:



* Dark Theme

* Light Theme



Theme switching should be instant and smooth.



Remember the user's preference.



Dark mode should be the primary design focus.



## Visual Style



Design language:



* Clean

* Minimal

* Premium

* Cinematic

* Modern

* Glassmorphism where appropriate

* Soft shadows

* Subtle gradients

* Excellent spacing

* Professional typography



The interface should feel expensive and carefully crafted.



## Color System



Dark Mode:



* Rich dark backgrounds

* Multiple surface layers

* Soft accent colors

* Excellent contrast



Light Mode:



* Clean white surfaces

* Subtle shadows

* Elegant muted colors



Avoid overly saturated colors.



Use accent colors sparingly.



## Typography



Use modern fonts such as:



* Inter

* Geist

* Manrope



Typography should create strong visual hierarchy.



Large bold headings.



Readable body text.



Perfect spacing between sections.



## Home Page



Create a stunning landing experience.



Include:



### Hero Section



Large title:



"Aman's Watch Vault"



Subtitle describing the collection.



Animated statistics.



Background effects.



Cinematic atmosphere.



### Featured Favorites Section



Large visually appealing cards.



Show:



* Favorite Anime

* Favorite Series



Make them the centerpiece of the page.



### Top 10 Section



Beautiful ranking layout.



Not a plain list.



Should feel special.



### Statistics Preview



Modern dashboard-style cards.



Animated counters.



## Navigation



Sticky navbar.



Glass effect while scrolling.



Theme toggle.



Smooth animations.



Elegant hover effects.



Mobile responsive.



## Media Cards



Cards should feel premium.



Include:



* Poster

* Title

* Rating

* Year



On hover:



* Scale slightly

* Elevation effect

* Glow effect

* Smooth transitions



Animations must feel smooth and expensive.



## Detail Page



When clicking a title:



Create a cinematic experience.



Include:



* Large banner image

* Poster

* Metadata

* Rating

* Genres

* Personal review



Layout should resemble a streaming platform detail page.



## Favorites Page



This should be one of the most beautiful pages.



Display favorites in a visually striking layout.



Make favorites feel important and prestigious.



## Statistics Page



Use modern analytics-inspired cards.



Display:



* Total Anime

* Total Series

* Average Rating

* Highest Rated Titles



Cards should feel interactive.



## Animations



Use tasteful animations only.



Examples:



* Fade in

* Slide up

* Hover elevation

* Smooth page transitions

* Staggered card animations



Avoid excessive motion.



Everything should feel refined.



## Mobile Experience



The mobile design must be first-class.



Not an afterthought.



The site should look amazing on:



* Phones

* Tablets

* Laptops

* Desktop monitors



## Final Goal



The final website should make visitors immediately think:



"Wow, this looks professionally designed."



The design should be portfolio-quality, highly polished, visually memorable, and significantly better than a standard CRUD application.









and not a single purple color in the UI
